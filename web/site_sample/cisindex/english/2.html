Features Courses Student counseling Career opportunities Goals and Future work

　

When the National Hualien University of Education's Department of Computer and Information Science was founded in 2006, the fledgling field was viewed as consisting of five primary areas:

(1)Computer Networking

    In this world, currents of information technology increasingly means that computer networking will continued be a popular domain. Our department aimed at the theories and practices of internet programming in this domain. The main courses included: Computer Networks, Internet Programming, Sever Installation and Management, Internet Computing and Applications, Wireless Networks, Data Communications and Network Security...etc.

(2)Database Systems and Data Mining

    Database systems and data mining is one of the foundations in this department. For expanding this area, we focus on theories and practices of database control systems module, data mining, and the applications in internet. The main courses included: Database Systems, File Structures, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, Optimization Theory, Fuzzy Logic and Neural Networks...etc.

(3)Multimedia Techniques

    In the field of Multimedia Techniques, our department focus on Multimedia design and application, theories and practices of internet media, image optimization and identifying. The main courses included: Multimedia System, Computer Graphics, Image Processing...etc.

(4)Design of Recreational softwares

    In the field of recreational softwares designing, we focus on Human-machine interface, Algorithmic recreational softwares, Role play theory, and Computer game software design. Besides the above-mentioned courses in multimedia techniques, we also provided the following courses: Introduction to Computer Game Software Design, Artificial Intelligence, Game Design, Virtual Reality, Theory and Practice of Vision Information...etc.

(5)Digital content

    Digital content industry has high developing potential. It is one of the super star industries in Taiwan. This domain inclued digital game, computer game, digital media, mobile application, e-learning, digital publication, content softwares, internet services...etc. We focus on Human-machine interface, Algorithmic recreational softwares, Role play theory, and Computer game software design. Besides the above-mentioned courses in multimedia techniques, we also provided the following courses: Introduction to Computer Game Software Design, Artificial Intelligence, Game Design, Virtual Reality, Theory and Practice of Vision Information...etc.

　

The courses are aimed to enhance students with abilities in both theories and practices.

Required courses:Calculus, Assembly Language, Digital Logic, Data structures, Special Project...etc.

Elective courses:Multimedia System, Data Mining, Wireless Networks, Human-Machine Interface, Data Communications, Sever Installation and Management, Neural Networks, Game Design...etc.

　

    We are short of high technology talents, this is a global problem. In a developed country, its developing direction would be IT industry, no matter EU, USA or Asia Pacific Zone. They fight for high technology talents. In terms of Taiwan's job market, IT industry provides nearly sixty percent of career opportunities. We are sure that our students will get jobs immediately after leaving school.

　

1. To prepare you to have the capability of doing Multimedia Calculus and its applications.

2. To prepare you as a computer scientist will also give you access to a wide range of jobs in software or website design and development.

3. To prepare you for careers in games creation companies.

4. To prepre you as a high technology talnet for local.

5. To pursue graduate studies in computer science or other related subjects, specializing in such diverse areas as artificial intelligence, hardware design, numerical analysis, software theory, or graphics.

　

    Our goals of the department are to cultivate students to be able to use the following techniques: on-line learning websites, internet teaching materials resources center, interactive multi-media design and production, on-line assessment system, internet database design, virtual reality, and teaching media design and production. In additional to the above, the department also uses computer technology, internet communication and visual-and-audio media in order to elevate the quality of education and to bring modern technology into the teaching of many subjects.

    As this department of Computer and Information Science is a new department in National Hualien University of Education. We train the students to explore a range of computer science theories and also some technique skill, to help them become experts in this field. We are also responsible for popularizing and guiding the domain of Computer and Information Science in the east part of Taiwan. In order to balance the gap between the city and the country, we would support relevant techniques and assist the school in the east part of Taiwan.
